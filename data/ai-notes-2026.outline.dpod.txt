	- Cursor's model selection
		- it has two modes: Auto and API
			- Auto: it selects from premium models (its own Composer, but also ChatGPT, Claude Opus, etc.)
				- there is no transparency
				- you can't blacklist models
				- however, thinking models like o3 are excluded
					- because slower response times and higher costs
			- API mode
				- you pick a specific model you want to use, e.g. Claude Opus
			- you can't pick o3, since it is available only in other plans
				- o3 is from OpenAI
